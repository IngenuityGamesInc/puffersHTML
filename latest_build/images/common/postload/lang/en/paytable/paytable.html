Welcome to the PUFFER GOES WILD Video Slot – themed upon the Marine Life with Wilds expanding to 2x2, 3x3 and 4x4 Larger Symbols during re-spins and many more exciting features.

ABOUT THE GAME

PUFFER GOES WILD is a 5-Reel Slot game, with each reel having 4 symbols in view, played with 178 ways.

PUFFER GOES WILD RE-SPINS BONUS

The re-spin bonus game starts when the Sharky and Puffer symbols appear on the first and fifth reel respectively. Puffer moves one position to the left during the re-spins, during the wins, the wins are paid and initiates another re-spin with Puffer moving one further Sharky on Reel 1 has the potential to expand the Puffer to create a 2x2 wild (extending back towards reel). Subsequent Sharky on reel 1 expands Puffer to 3x3 and 4x4 large symbol. Puffer wilds landing on reel 5 during re-spins will act as swimming wilds and follow the initial swimming wild triggered. Sharky on reel 1 will expand swimming puffer individually, so combos of 1x1, 2x2, 3x3 , 4x4 are achievable.

OTHER GAME INFO

  • Coinciding wins on different ways are added together and paid to player.

  • Coin Value – Displays the theoretical stake prize of the current setting. All wins are shown in coins which should be multiplied by the coin value to convert to a Cash Prize win.

  • Bet – Shows the value of bet of the current setting. The bet size is 1 coin multiplied by the bet multiplier.

  • Max Bet (Desktop ONLY) – Sets the coin value to the largest available.

  • Spin Button – Starts the game. When the reels are spinning, the spin button transforms into the Turbo Stop Button. Press the Turbo Stop Button to stop the reels immediately.

  • Autoplay – You can set the game to play without needing to press the spin button each time. Choose the number of rounds to auto play by pressing the Autoplay button (or by holding down the Spin Button to open the Autoplay Menu). Where applicable you can choose additional conditions for when to stop autoplay in the Game Settings. The loss limit in some game client versions prevents you from losing above set limit during your autoplay session. To stop autoplay manually, press the Stop Autoplay button.

  • Win – Displays the win for the current or last win paid.

  • Cash/Coin – Displays your account balance as either Cash (monetary value) or Coins (number of credits) depending on the choice of the player. The default display is Cash.

RETURN TO PLAYER

  • The overall theoretical return to player is 96-96.5%. 

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

  • All winning combinations are paid left to right on a payline, with each starting at Reel 1, except scatter combinations, which may occur in any position.

  • Winnings achieved within Features are paid in addition to ways combinations.

  • In the event of malfunction of the gaming hardware/software, all affected game bets and payouts are rendered void and all affected bets refunded.
